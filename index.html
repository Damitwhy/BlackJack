BLACKJACK RULES

To beat the dealer and win, you need to...

Be dealt with a Blackjack* or hold more points in your hand than the dealer

The dealer will continue to take cards until they have 17 or more

Don't get more than 21 because you will bust and lose!

If you win you'll have 1 added to your score

If you win with Blackjack* you'll have 2 added to your score

And if the dealer wins they'll have 1 added to their score

Draw 2 cards of the same kind you can split and play 2 hands for twice the fun!
